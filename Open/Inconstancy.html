Inconstancy

The woman that symbolizes Inconstancy is unsteadily standing on a wheel, which is sliding down a sloped floor. As the flying cape indicates, Giotto depicts this woman while she is falling on her back. This intends to symbolize the fact that as soon as an indecent person is led into temptation, s/he falls onto it.

Artist: Giotto

Dating: 1303-1305

Medium: Fresco

Dimension:120x55cm (47,2x21,6in)

Vice Number: Sixth

Opposite Virtue: Fortitude
